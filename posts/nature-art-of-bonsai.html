The Art Of Bonsai


Published 24 August 2023




Introduction


Bonsai, those tiny trees grown in pots that seem too small. Most of the tiny trees you can find look way more beautiful and 'perfect' than a regular tree or shrub found in nature. This is by design. Bonsai is an art form where one controls and cares for the growth of a tree in a small pot, one that is thousands of years old at this point. Bonsai is the japanese word for a chinese art form thats been around since at least 700 AD, where the oldest tomb paintings depict the first bonsai (although the art form must be much older, since the paintings show 'fully-grown' bonsai).
