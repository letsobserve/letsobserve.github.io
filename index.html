More About Observe


This is idea started back in 2019, leading to the creation of a YouTube Channel-Observation where I could show off the beauty of the natural world. Fast-foward to today and the evolution of this idea into a website that can further explore natural phenomena, like nature, food and dreaming- which I feel most passionate towards.


This website is completely coded from scratch. No website builder, no out-sourcing, just a text editor and a lot of googling. It was important to me to be able to make this myself, having complete control to be able put my heart and soul into it and being able to continually learn and expand my knowledge of website development.
